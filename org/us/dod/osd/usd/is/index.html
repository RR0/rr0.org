L'Under-Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security est l'USD délégué au Renseignement et la Sécurité.

Le 2021-11-23, Kathleen Hicks (Secrétaire Adjointe à la Défense), en collaboration étroite avec le DNI, directed the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence & Security to establish within the Office of the USD(I&S) the Airborne Object Identification and Management Synchronization Group (AOIMSG) as the successor to the U.S. Navy’s Unidentified Aerial Phenomena Task Force.
