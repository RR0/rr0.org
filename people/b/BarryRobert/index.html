"Bob" Barry s'intéresse à l'ufologie à partir de l'observation d'Arnold.

En 1957-11, il dirige la 2ème Conférence Ovni pour Western New York (Olean, NY). the first having been held in March of that same year. The event featured several taped interviews by Barry, including U.S. Rep. Kenneth B. Keating, former Secretary of the U.S. Air Force Thomas K. Finletter, et Truman Bethurum. The event was so successful that they were forced to turn 20-30 persons away. Taking advantage of the interest, the Aerial Phenomenon Investigation Society (APIS) was formed on 1958-01-09 with Barry as its director. A third UFO conference, the first officially hosted by APIS, was held on 1959-05-15 and featured contactee Laura Mundo. Newspaper reports made it clear that she was permitted to tell her tale on account of expressed interest but that APIS did not necessarily confirm the validity of contactee reports.

Il enquête pour diverses associations ufologiques et dirige le 20th Century UFO Bureau. Il publie un pamphlet Barry, R.: Ufo's -- What's Ahead?, 20th Century UFO Bureau, 1975-01-01 sur l'ancienne ufologie et la prophécie biblique.

Le 1975-10-27 Barry prédit, Le gouvernement nous dira ce qui s'est passé dans une série de documentaires télévisés sur une période de plusieurs mois... Toute l'histoire est prévue pour être révélée au 200ème anniversaire de l'Indépendance le 1976-07-04 "The Cloudy Crystal Ball" Skeptic UFO Newsletter #14 1992-03 p. 8.

Barry est invité dans diverses émissions radio et télévisées. Il crée la sienne sur le satellite : E.T. Monitor.
