Consultant privé with more than 40 years of experience in the intelligence community and defense industry. Held senior positions at ManTech International Corp. and was VP for intelligence at Litton. Il passe plus de 33 ans au sein du gouvernement américain, ayant rejoint le CIA in 1964 in its newly formed Foreign Missile and Space Analysis Center. During his CIA career he served in various roles, including director of weapons intelligence, associate deputy director of intelligence, and director of science and technology.

Il fut évoqué comme "Everett" Hineman dans la conversation qu'eût l'amiral Inman avec Robert Oechsler, et qui détiendrait d'importantes informations au sujet de l'implication des États-Unis avec le sujet ovni.
