He and his colleague, David Moyer, volunteered to consult with the project on matters related to UFO effects on automobile engines. They worked on one case which was reported in the final report, one case which essentially was not, and were not employed to do other work on cases even though Hooven volunteered to do so. Hooven continued to be interested in the phenomenon after the Project's end and believed that it deserved good study. Roy F.
