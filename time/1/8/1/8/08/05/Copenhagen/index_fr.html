Vendredi dernier, 17, vers 19:00 dans la soirée, des voyageurs observèrent un phénomène naturel remarquable à mi-chemin entre Soendburg et Odensee, dans Fulmen. There fell from the air like rain, an incredible number of larger and smaller balls, which, as they passed through the sunbeams, assumed all the colours of the rainbow. These balls, when they were caught, dissolved in vapour, and left yellow sports on the hands, and a strong smell of sulphur. They danced in the air like soap bubbles. This phenomenon continued nearly till sunset.
