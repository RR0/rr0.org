Clark, J. E.: "shape-shifting entity, Ohio, 1866", Magonia Exchange, 2008-06-04

The Cincinnati Gazette says considerable excitement exists in the neighborhood of Stepping Stone Landing on account of a supposed apparition that makes its appearance in that vicinity. It is said to assume a variety of shapes – at times flourishing a dagger, and then appearing as a horse with a human head. The crew of the Clermont and Magnolia have both been favored with a sight at this phenomenon, which[,] unlike most of its family, appears by daylight.
