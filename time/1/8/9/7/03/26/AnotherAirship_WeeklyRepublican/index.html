A Mysterious Light Moves Over San Jose.

SAN JOSE, March 19. - Now that the all absorbing question of the world's heavyweight championship has been determined, the airship mystery is again attracting attention. A pale white light moving with great rapidity over this city tonight has revived the theme, and many who saw the phenomenon are confident that the problem of air navigation has been solved.
