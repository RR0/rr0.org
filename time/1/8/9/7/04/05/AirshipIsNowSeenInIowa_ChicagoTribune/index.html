People at Albia See a Brilliant Reddish Light Coming from the West And Moving North

Albia, Ia., April 4. - {Special} - The mysterious airship seen so often in Kansas passed over this city about 10 o'clock last night, cam e from the west, and then it passed toward the north, moving in an erratic manner. It appeared as a brilliant reddish light, and was seen by many reliable people.
