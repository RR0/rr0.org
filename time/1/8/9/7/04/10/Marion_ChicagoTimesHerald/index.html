Article du Chicago Times-Herald

The Chicago Times-Herald de Chicago (Illinois), samedi 10 avril 1897

Home > Documents > Articles > Airships | Traduction française

Marion, Iowa, April 9, - An air ship passed in a southerly direction over this town at 2:05 o'clock this morning. It was seen by the night operators of the Western Union Telegraph Company and others. It appeared to be about 500 feet above the ground. The outlines were distinctly visible against the sky. The ship gave out two bright lights of an electric glow.
