Article du Chicago Times-Herald

The Chicago Times-Herald de Chicago (Illinois), samedi 10 avril 1897

Home > Documents > Articles > Airships | Traduction française

Wausau, Wis., April 9. – What was supposed to be an air ship was seen passing over this city last night about 10 o'clock. Lights could be distinguished from a dark object far up in the heavens, which had the appearance of being shaped like an egg. It was viewed by at least fifty persons.
