Burlington Hawkeye Plays An Airship Trick On The Populace

The Champaign Daily Gazette de Champaign (Illinois), lundi 12 avril 1897

Home > Documents > Articles > Airships | Traduction française

Burlington, In., April 12 – In order to test the credulity of the people on the airship question, members of the Hawkeye's staff sent up a common tissue paper hot air balloon outside the city Saturday night so that the wind would carry it over the town. The test was eminently satisfactory and the Hawkeye's telephone was kept hot all evening with messages describing the airship, some people swearing they saw red and green lights, and one perfectly sober and reputable citizen offering to make affidavit that he heard voices. The experiment satisfies The Hawkeye that the Nebraska-Iowa-Illinois airship is a pure fake.
