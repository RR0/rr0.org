Clark, J. E.: "airship and occupants, Illinois, 1897", Magonia Exchange, 25 septembre 2007

The following telegram was received in Waterloo this morning:

LANARK, Ill., April 12. – The airship came down here last night. Two of the occupants are dead. All were foreigners. The Chicago & Northwestern has run a special from Freeport loaded with sight-seers.
