The Airship Sails in the Northwestern Heavens and is Seen by Many people

Bancroft Locals

Bancroft, April 12. – There must be a whole regiment of airships in this neck of the woods. Responsible citizens of Bancroft, Ledyard and Armstrong claim to have seen the critter last Saturday night [10 avril]. Dr. C.B. Paul claims he could see the light on both ends, and a colored one and the other clear like the headlight of a locomotive. My but these spring clouds do look queer!
