It Blew Up and Went to Pieces Before It was Seen in Battle Creek

Galesburg, April 13 ? George W. Somers and William Chadburn of Pavilion Township claim the alleged airship went to pieces in that vicinity Sunday night [11 avril]. They heard a dull explosion and the lights on the supposed airship went out. The next morning, it is said that part of the propeller and some wire were found which proved that the thing had burst.
