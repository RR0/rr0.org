Article du State Republican

The State Republican de Lansing (Michigan), mercredi 14 avril 1897

Home > Documents > Articles > Airships | Traduction française

The airship was plainly seen by several reliable citizens of Benton Harbor and St. Joseph ? so they allege ? and declare that it is no fake. It was seen about 8:00 Tuesday night [13 avril], with blue, red, and green lights, and was moving rapidly in a northwesterly direction. The outlines could not be discerned, owing to the lights it bore and its great altitude over Lake Michigan.
