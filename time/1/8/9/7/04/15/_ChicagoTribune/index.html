The Chicago Tribune de Chicago (Illinois), jeudi 15 avril 1897

Tuesday [13 avril] night was a busy one for the airship. It exploded in Kalamazoo, Mich., ran aground in Carlinville, Ill., and made its debut in Washington, D.C. It now seems to be a three-ringed affair.
