The Mysterious Airship Makes Its Appearance – Heard Them Talk.

Dunkirk, O., April 17. – The mysterious airship passed here. It was seen in the eastern part of town, within 800 feet of the ground. Marshal Mahon and others heard passengers in the airship talking loudly.

At Casstown, O., Farmer James McKenzie reports that the airship passed over his farm yesterday.
