Occupants of the Airship Sing John Howard Payne's Popular Song.

Berea, May 7. – That wonderful airship was seen Wednesday night [May 5] by some of our society ladies, who described the different kinds of animals upon it. They further say that guitar and banjo music was accompanying the singing of "Home, Sweet Home." There are others.
