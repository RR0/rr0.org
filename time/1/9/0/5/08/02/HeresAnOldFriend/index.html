Here's An Old Friend

Star Journal de Sandusky (Ohio), 2 août 1905 Clark, J. E.: "airship, Ohio, 1905", 16 novembre 2007

Home | Traduction française

COSHOCTON, Ohio, Aug. 2. – Clarence Hay, a local jeweler, and two companions say they saw an airship sail over Walhonding, a village 10 miles above here, last night, bound southeastward and showing three red lights astern and one green at the bow. They think it to be the airship that Chicago papers have gossiped of making the journey from Chicago to Washington.
