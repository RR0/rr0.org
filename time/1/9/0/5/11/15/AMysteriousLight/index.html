Clark, J.: "ghost light, Alberta, 1905 and before", Magonia Exchange, 2007-08-29

Several of the people living around eight-mile lake are again trying to solve the problem of the mysterious light that has been visible, so it is stated, for many years back. The light is easily discernable in an easterly direction from Captain McDonald's house and that gentleman and a party of visitors started from his ranch one night recently to locate the ghostly glim [?]. They traveled a considerable distance, but as they still seemed to be as far away from the source as ever, gave the investigation up. Many persons claim to have seen this strangely elusive light, but when you ask them what they think it is or whence it comes[,] they all shake their heads and say, "I pass." Possibly it is of a phosphorescent nature, although this theory is contradicted by some.
