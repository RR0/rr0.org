Passed Over Hampton Roads Occupied by Man and Woman.

Norfolk, Va., May 3. – The spectacle of a mysterious airship, occupied by a man and a woman, passing over Sewell's point, was witnessed by more than fifty persons in that vicinity.

Those who saw the strange air craft stated that it resembled an automobile without wheels.
