Roderick, Dyke: "airships, cryptid, Ontario, 1909", Magonia Exchange, 2007-11-09

(Ottawa Citzen)

Fiction is losing a leading light in the correspondent of the Associated Press at Woodstock. A few months ago he had [train robber] Bill Miner lurking in the fastnesses of Oxford county; later he had mysterious flying machines hovering over the Zorras, and now some sort of mastodon "with feet as big as a man's head" is terrorizing the sturdy yeomanry of Sweaburg. Evidently to this young man, truth is stranger than fiction.
