Theo Paijmans: Magonia Exchange, 29 avril 2007

The river police observed monday night at around 4 o'clock on the river de Waal near Tiel in northern direction a beautiful natural phenomenon. The dark sky was suddenly brightly illumined, a big fireball appeared not shortly thereafter, that blew apart at having reached a height of approximately 40 degrees, after which the sparks rained down as a column of fire. The phenomenon lasted long enough for it to be observed well.
