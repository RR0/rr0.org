Chattanooga Residents Aroused by Third View of Specter.

Chattanooga, Tenn., Jan. 15. -- For the third successive day a mysterious white air craft passed over Chattanooga about noon yesterday. It came from the north and was traveling southeast, disappearing over Missionary Ridge. Wednesday it came south and Thursday it returned north.
