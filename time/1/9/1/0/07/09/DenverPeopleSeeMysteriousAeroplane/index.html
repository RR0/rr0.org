Clark, J. E.: "mystery airplane, Colorado, 1910", Magonia Exchange, mercredi 31 octobre 2007

DENVER, Colo., July 9. – According to former Judge W. N. Dixon and member of his family an aeroplane passed over Denver last night. They say that the machine was flying about 1,000 feet in the air and at a terrific speed. Denver men interested in aerial navigation say they do not know of any machine in this vicinity capable of making such a flight.
