Strange Airship Is Nocturnal New York Wonder.

New York, Aug. 31. – A score or more of persons in Madison Square park, about 9 o'clock last night, saw far above the city to the east two bright lights, one green and the other blue. They were wide apart and arranged like the port and starboard lights of a ship under way. Then only the green light shows, soaring to the north. The spectators rubbed their eyes and began to punch themselves. Just then the red light came into view again and the two, like the eyes of some monster bird, bore directly toward the park, as if to alight in the square.

While the bluecoats [police] and the others began to doubt their vision[,] they heard a low, buzzing noise that soon became a whirr, then a roar, accompanied by explosions like the reports from a rapid fire gun. There was no doubt of it any more - it was an airship.

The machine circled around the Metropolitan tower and then disappeared. No one knows the name of the aviator who guided the mysterious airship.
