Aeroplane Flies Over New York Skyscrapers

Daily News de Des Moines (Iowa), 4 septembre 1910 Clark, J. E.: "mystery airplane, New York, 1910", Magonia Exchange, mercredi 31 octobre 2007

Home | Traduction française

NEW YORK, Sept. 3. – Thousands of people in the vicinity of Madison Square park were amazed by a mysterious aeroplane which came from the direction of Long Island and hovered over the city for at least forty-five minutes. The strange aerial visitor was about twenty feet wide, patterned after the familiar Wright or Curtiss type, and carried a red and green light on the port and starboard wings. It circled about the Flatiron Building, soared high above the Metropolitan Life Insurance building tower and finally swooped down within 800 feet of Madison Square park. Then slowly it disappeared toward Long Island Sound.
