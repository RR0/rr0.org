Flood, Chuck: "'SAW AIRSHIP AT GRASSY LAKE'; Lethbridge (Alberta) Herald, November 2, 1910", Magonia Exchange, 2007-08-20

Grassy Lake, Nov. 1 -- Peter Theriot who is doing homestead duties near Badwater Lake, came into town yesterday and claims that he was very much frightened by an object in the heavens, just at daybreak Thursday morning, while looking for his horses on the prairie. This is supposed to be the airship seen by others about the same time.
