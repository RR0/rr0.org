Clark, J.: "airship, California, 1914", Magonia Exchange, 2007-08-16

SAN BERNARDINO, Nov. 24. - Coming out of the desert like a phantom, a mysterious airship is nightly hurtling through space, terrifying the few inhabitants and puzzling the telegraph operators along the railway lines.

Twice these night men at the stations have heard the rhythmic hunt [hum?] of the ghost-like aircraft, and midnight vigils are now being kept in the hope that the strange visitor might be sighted.

From Helen, Hesperia and Victorville come reports that the mysterious craft, flying low, but still unseen, has passed over those points.

So the residents of the desert border land are becoming alarmed, for they know not what to fear.

Out from the great gray desert comes the strange midnight aircraft, back to the desert it flies. But whether it comes and whither it goes - that is the query on every lip along the airship's trail.
