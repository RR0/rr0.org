Clark, J.: "airships, New York, 1914, 1915", Magonia Exchange, 2008-08-15

[excerpt]

Another airship was seen and heard Saturday [January 2] by several West Martinsburg people. We all know how stormy it was that day, and it seems strange that the aeronauts prefer traveling in cold weather. This is the ninth airship which has been known to pass through this section of country since September.
