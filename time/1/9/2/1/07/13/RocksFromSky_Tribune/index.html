Clark, J.: "fall of rocks, New York, 1921", Magonia Exchange, 2007-07-29

UTICA, N.Y., July [illegible]. - A house on a lonely road near here in which Mr. and Mrs. William Ashley and their family recently moved is the scene of a mysterious phenomenon. For several weeks stones as large as billiard balls have been dropping on the house, apparently out of a clear sky. They fall perpendicularly, as if dropped, not thrown. All efforts to explain their source have been vain.
