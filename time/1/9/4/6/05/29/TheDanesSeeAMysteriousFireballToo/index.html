The Danes see a mysterious fireball too

Morgon-Tidningen de Stockholm (Suède), 29 mai 1946, p. 7 Bartholomew, Robert: Magonia Exchange, 10 mai 2007

Home

COPENHAGEN, Tuesday (AP). The same night that people in Landskrona saw a flying object which flew toward the southwest at about 100 m. altitude, a border guard in Denmark's southern-lying parish, Rudbbl, at Tonder, made a similar discovery.

At 3 o'clock at night the guard saw a bright light, followed by a tail, which very rapidly sailed on a path from east to west, reports the Herlingske Aftenavis.
