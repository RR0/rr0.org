Flaming Red Meteor In Southern Finland

Bartholomew, Robert: Magonia Exchange, 10 mai 2007

At 22:20 o'clock on Thursday was observed again a strange luminous phenomenon in the sky. It was also obvious that a meteor was concerned. The luminosity, which was plainly perceptible against the light summer evening, moved slowly forward in a direction from south to north and flamed red. According to the observers' opinion the meteor ought to have fallen down somewhere in the Pellinge area of Nylands Island.
