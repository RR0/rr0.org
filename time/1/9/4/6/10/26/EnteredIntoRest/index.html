Clark, J. E.: "Adamski's mother, 1946", Magonia Exchange, 1er octobre 2007

ADAMSKI: Mrs. Frances, Thursday, Oct. 24th, 1946 at the residence of her son, Sylvester Adamski, 126 Serval street. Born in Poland, resident of this city, for the past 50 years. Member of St. Hedwig's church and St. Peters society.

Mother of George Adamski of Center Valley [sic], Cal., Mrs. Lena Bialaszweski, Mrs. Walter Papierski, Walter and Sylvester Adamski of this city and Mrs. Vincent Filipowski of Lackawanna. Brother of John Kwiatkowski of Detroit, Mich. Fourteen grandchildren and 4 great-grandchildren also survive.

Funeral services from the residence of her daughter, Mrs. Lena Bialaszewski, 332 Nevin street, Monday, Oct. 28th, 1946 at 8:30 and 9 o'clock in St. Hedwig's church. Interment to follow in the family plot in the parish cemetery.

Supkoski Funeral Service
