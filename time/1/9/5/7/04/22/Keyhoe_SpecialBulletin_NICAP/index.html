HEAD OF NACA BACKS DOWN ON FLYING SAUCER CLAIM. On March 14 Dr. Hugh Dryden, Director National Advisory Committee fo Aeronautics, was publicly quoted: "Flying saucers do not exist." In answer Director Keyhoe wrote Dr. Dryden: "If you can provide absolute proof that UFO's are non-existent, I shall request the Board to dissolve this Committee at once."

In reply Dr. Dryden said he had denied reports that the Nazis had a flying saucer in 1945. (Keyhoe had already denied this story.) Next day, interviewed by WIP Philadelphia, Dr. Dryden was asked if important information on other flying saucer possibilities was withheld by the Government. Dr. Dryden: "No comment." In a second NICAP letter he was asked if he still denied existence of all flying saucers, or merely the German rumor. Replying by telegram, Dr. Dryden flatly refused to answer.
