Great Falls, Mont. (UPI)—Several persons Tuesday night reported seeing unidentified flying objects over Great Falls, Choteau, Vaughn, Fort Benton, and Manchester.

The objects, described as white lights—a big one with a little one apparently on top—moved east to west, but occasionally would stop then go north and south.

The sightings in the five communities were made between 7:45 and 8:30 p.m.
