Incidence of Time-Proximity to the UE on the Biochemical Variability

The homogeneity of the distribution of biochemical parameters along the ecological axis may have been altered by an external factor. The variances calculated for the five samples at D + 40 and D + 730, respectively, have been compared by the "F" test. Significant differences have been noticed in the following 8 cases:

Lutein: F=11.5 P(F)=0.02
Glucose: F=9.3 P(F)=0.03
