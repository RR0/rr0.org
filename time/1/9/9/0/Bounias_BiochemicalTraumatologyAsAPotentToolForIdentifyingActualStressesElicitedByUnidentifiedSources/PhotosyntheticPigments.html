These coefficients significantly deviate from 1 in both young (p = 4.81) and old leaves (p = 11,99). Dans l'échantillon C (tableau 2), levels are decreased by an average factor C/G - 0. 5 8 relative to sample G, considered as reference. This factor, already significant (N = 5; p = 0.056), drops to 0.48 if chlorophyll B (particularly more stable than chlorophyll A) is discarded (N = 4; p = 0.043). In samples H to L, no statistically significant differences could be found.
