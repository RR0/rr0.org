It has been shown that the algebraic form of the relations between two biochemical parameters may prove to be a sensitive way for evidencing stress effects (Bounias, 1975; Bounias et al.,1987). This might allow the characterization of stresses even in particular cases where no deviation of the concentrations from the normal range of natural values can be detected.
