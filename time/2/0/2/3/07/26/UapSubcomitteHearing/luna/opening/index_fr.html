Les circonstances entourant les PANs ont capté l'attention du peuple américain depuis des décennies, ancré dans l'esprit même des dirigeants de notre nation, de Jimmy Carter à Barack Obama, de Hillary Clinton à Donald Trump, de Marco Rubio à Chuck Schumer, de John Radcliffe à des représentants du Conseil de Sécurité Nationale. Pourtant, depuis Roswell, New Mexico jusqu'à la côte de Jacksonville, en Floride, les observations de PANs ont rarement été expliquées par les gens ayant des témoignages de première main de ces situations.

Ceci est largement dû au manque de transparence de notre gouvernement et l'échec de nos dirigeants élus à tenir leurs promesses de publier les explications et films et montagnes de documents sur-classifiés qui continuent d'être cachés au peuple américain. C'est juste le contraire de ce à quoi je crois. En fait, le peuple américain est largement convaincu que le gouvernement a activement dissimulé la vérité sur les PANs.

Un sondage en particulier a révélé que 68 % des Américains pensent que le gouvernement cache des informations sur les PANs et n'est pas honnête sur ce qu'il sait à leur sujet. Et de mon expérience personnelle, je pense la même chose. Un autre sondage a trouvé que pratiquement la moitié des Américains pense que le gouvernement fédéral gère mal ou plutôt mal les signalements d'ovnis.

Comme le représentant Burchett vient d'y faire référence on the FA bill that just went through, you can tell that that’s exactly happening. Considering the thousands of testimonies and videos taken on people’s phones and eyewitnesses accounts made by credible witnesses such as doctors, pilots, scientists, and active duty service members. It is unacceptable to continue to gaslight Americans into thinking that this is not happening or that the potential of intelligent life forms exist other than humans.

Even more alarming is the fact that these eyewitnesses are many of times service members and have no assurance that their lives will not be negatively impacted or even harmed by their experiences. In being an active duty service member working on an airfield, I’ve had conversations with many pilots where they were in fear of coming forward for retribution and or being taken off flight status. How do we know this? Because the government has said nothing to assure us otherwise.

They have also did nothing to calm the concerns of over 20% of Americans who have reported to have seen UFOs or UAPs. We are simply told not to question the government and that the government has it under control. Today is the first hearing of its kind where we will attempt to get down to the bottom of what is actually happening with UAPs, but we will hear from people who have had personal sightings rather than Pentagon bureaucrats who have always been sent to Stonewaller Investigations, just so that the press knows and the people know.

We were even denied access to a classified briefing in a SCIF prior to this hearing due to the amount of hoops that we had to jump through to grant temporary clearance to witness Grusch who has knowledge of classified information. It is time to have an open-minded discussion on this topic, to hear the evidence and understand the magnitude of what this means, not just for our nation, but for humanity. Thank you, Chairman, I yield back the rest of my time.
